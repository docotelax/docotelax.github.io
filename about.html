The Story Behind Docotela X

"Born from struggle. Inspired by freedom. Built to last."

Docotela X was born from the idea that true freedom comes from creating without compromise. Our founder spent years crafting a custom 1978 VW Beetle, building every inch from a bare shell — proving consistency, patience, and vision. That same energy now drives every collection we release.

Our Journey

2019

Docotela X was just an idea scribbled in a notebook.

2021

The Beetle build begins — a symbol of resilience and vision.

2023

“Freedom as a Feeling” is born. Design takes a new direction.

2025

Launch of Docotela X. Limited collections meet pure storytelling.
