About Docotela X

Docotela X is more than a brand — it’s a movement.

Born from the desire to express freedom through craftsmanship, Docotela X invites the world into the soul of independent artistry. Each piece is handcrafted, limited, and infused with the spirit of rebellion against the ordinary.

Founded on the philosophy that fashion is wearable art, our collections are deeply personal — a mirror of the creator’s journey and the wearer's sense of identity.

From the first sketch to the final stitch, our goal is simple: make you feel something real.
