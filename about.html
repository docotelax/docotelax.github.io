About Docotela X

"Docotela X is more than a brand. It’s a rebellion. A feeling. A movement of art expressed through the craft of fashion."

Born from the struggle of chasing freedom and the courage to live with intention, Docotela X exists to embody a lifestyle of fearless expression. Each piece reflects our journey — raw, poetic, and unfiltered.

We don’t just design clothing. We design experiences. From the soul of a 1978 VW Beetle rebuilt with vision, to collections crafted by young artists, our message is clear: authenticity is freedom.

Welcome to the story. Welcome to the feeling. Welcome to Docotela X.
