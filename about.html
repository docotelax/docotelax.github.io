Our Story

Docotela X was born from a desire to craft fashion that speaks to freedom, identity, and expression. From handbuilt classics like a 1978 VW Beetle to the creation of art-infused garments, every thread and every detail has a purpose. The vision was never just about clothing — it was about a feeling. A movement. A truth.

Timeline of Moments

  • 2019: The Beetle project begins – a black shell turned dream machine.
  • 2021: Countless design drafts, failures, and breakthroughs spark inspiration.
  • 2023: The concept of freedom finds form in art and fashion.
  • 2025: Docotela X is launched – exclusive, handcrafted, and story-driven.
