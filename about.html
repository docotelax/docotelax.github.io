Our Journey

Docotela X was born from a pursuit of freedom. Every piece tells a story of resilience, creativity, and artistic rebellion. Our collections are more than garments — they are experiences handwoven by young artists to reflect the soul of our journey.

2018 - The Idea Sparked

A dream took form. Freedom became the core philosophy. From sketchbooks to vision boards, the concept for Docotela X was born.

2022 - Building the Machine

After years of doubt and rejection, we built our 1978 VW Beetle from a shell — proving our consistency, vision, and patience. It became a symbol of the brand.

2025 - The Rise of Docotela X

Now we launch our collections, handmade by ten artists, with each piece limited, crafted, and curated to evoke the feeling of freedom.
